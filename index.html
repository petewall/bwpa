About the Woods

The Baihly Woods Preservation Association (BWPA) was established in 1997 when 15 original members, owning adjoining properties, purchased a 10.2 acre parcel of woodland.

This woodland is a cherished natural space, protected and stewarded by our community through a local land trust. We are dedicated to preserving its biodiversity, ensuring access for future generations, and providing a place for peace, exploration, and learning.
